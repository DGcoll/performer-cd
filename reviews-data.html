Title of the paper : Morpho-Energetic Indicators Method Coupled with GIS for Built Urban Environment Energy Management List of authors : Khadidja Rahmani Nahla Bouaziz Asma Bouattou Abstract : Facing the climate change and ecological disastrous, urban areas and their organiza-tion have an important effect on energy consumption reduction. In this paper, we are studying the relationship between morphological indicators and energy consumption in Miliana city, which located 199 km southwest of Algiers, and their impact on thermo-aerolic comfort by using simulation soft-ware for thermo-aerodynamic comfort assessing and geographic information system (GIS) for specialization and visualization of the results in the form of mapping cards. The results of this study have shown that urban design influences themo-aeraulic comfort and energy consumption. It may also contribute to build an urban environ-ment management. Download
